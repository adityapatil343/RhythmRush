How to Play

  • Tap the black tiles as they cross the gray hit line near the bottom.
  • Correct hit = +1 point. Tiles light up when hit.
  • Wrong tap (tapping where there is no tile) or missing a tile ends the game.
  • Controls: tap with finger, click with mouse. Press Space to start/pause on keyboard.

How to Play

  • Tap or click the dark tiles when they cross the blue hit line.
  • A correct tap adds a point; tapping empty space or missing a tile ends the game.
  • Use the Space key to start or pause the game.
  • Try the Theme button to switch between light and dark modes.
